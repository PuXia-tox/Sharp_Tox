## PhD & Research Opportunities

The **SHARP Tox Lab (Systems Hazard Assessment & Risk Prediction)** welcomes students and collaborators interested in:

- Computational toxicology
- AOP network modelling and mechanistic interpretation
- High-throughput omics for hazard assessment
- Cross-species toxicology (zebrafish, Daphnia, Drosophila and human systems)

If you are interested in PhD positions or visiting opportunities, please contact: **p.xia@bham.ac.uk**
