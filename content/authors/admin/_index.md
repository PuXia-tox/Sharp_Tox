Pu Xia is an Assistant Professor in Precision Toxicology at University of Birmingham. His research interests include development and application of omics-based New Approach Methodologies, AI-based predictive toxicology and comparative toxicology. He leads the Sharp_Tox group, which develops high-throughput omics (transcriptomics and phenomics) approaches, human organ-on-chip, AOP network-based modelling to predict chemical toxicity.
